What is Monoly Trust Network?

In B2B communication service market, a new paradigm of technology is required to meet the growing demand for data privacy and productivity.

Monoly is the enterprise trust network that enables both internal and external entities to communicate and collaborate. Eliminating the risk of service providers’ access to users’ data, Monoly not only facilitates enterprises to communicate safely with teams, partners or clients but also increases productivity without sacrificing data privacy.
